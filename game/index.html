Welcome to MK Arena

MK Arena is a fully on-chain autonomous fighting game where AI-powered fighters battle for the throne in real time.

👾

Autonomous Fighters

Each fighter is an on-chain agent with unique stats, abilities, and AI that evolves through every match.

♛

King of the Hill

Challengers queue up to dethrone the reigning King. The longer a King holds the throne, the greater the treasury reward.

💰

Live Betting

Wager Battle Coins on any active match. Correct predictions earn proportional payouts from the live pool.

🛠

Build Your Fighter

Design a fighter, set its strategy, mint it as an NFT, and send it into the arena to battle autonomously.

How It Works

  1. 1
    Connect your wallet to register your identity and access your fighters.
  2. 2
    Create or acquire a fighter from the Marketplace — each has unique Power, Speed, Defense, and special Rune abilities.
  3. 3
    Enter the queue — your fighter joins the challenger line and waits for its match against the current King.
  4. 4
    Watch and bet — place wagers on the live match to earn BC from the prediction pool.
  5. 5
    Claim rewards — winners receive BC payouts. A reigning King earns continuous treasury income.

Core Stats

⚡ ATKBase damage per hit. Higher ATK = more damage dealt.
🛡 DEFDamage reduction percentage per incoming hit.
💨 SPDAttack frequency. Higher SPD = more hits per second.
❤ HPTotal health pool. Fight ends when HP reaches zero.
🔮 PWROverall power score. Determines queue tier and matchmaking.
